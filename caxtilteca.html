About the Game

Caxtilteca is a 4 player mini RTS set in colonial Mexico. Play as Tlaxcalan dukes defending themselves against their former friends and allies for land. The only way to do this is to organize your army of peasants, knights, and towers to the most efficient arrangement for money collection while also constantly juggling your opponents who have the exact same goal.

Experience messy pulse pounding multiplayer in the Battle Theatre, reenacting old battles with up to four friends. Master a treacherous landscape in single player Attack or Defense Scenarios, each posing unique challenges and each part of a weaved together story.

Backstory

Long ago, before the Spanish Conquista, a "secret" deal was struck. The Caxtilteca (the Spaniards) would capture their prize, and the tens of thousands who helped would became citizens, not slaves. A great city would be founded for them to rule- the Loyal City of Tlaxcala. They even sailed across the ocean and were granted freedom from tribute from the King himself.

But when push comes to shove, how long do friendships really last?
